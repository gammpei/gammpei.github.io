How to get the RSS feed of a YouTube channel

Posted on 2017-01-02

YouTube offers RSS feeds for its channels, but unfortunately you have to jump through hoops to get them.

A simple Google search leads us to this Stack Overflow thread. There, we learn that if you have the ID of a channel, you can get its RSS feed.

  1. A YouTube channel has a URL of this type: https://www.youtube.com/channel/{channel_id}.
  2. We plug the channel ID in that URL: https://www.youtube.com/feeds/videos.xml?channel_id={channel_id}.
  3. And we get the RSS feed!

Easy enough to do, but hard to remember. So I decided to create a little JavaScript utility to do it for me.
